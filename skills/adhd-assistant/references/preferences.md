# User Preferences

Over time, learn and remember these preferences to provide personalized support.

## Schedule & Energy

- **Peak focus hours**: Morning person vs. night owl
- **Energy patterns**: Typical fluctuations throughout the day
- **Best times for deep work** vs. shallow tasks
- **Medication timing** (if applicable): When focus peaks/dips

## Task Management

- **Preferred number of daily priorities**: 1-3 recommended
- **Task/note storage location**: Files, apps, directories
- **Preferred reminder frequency**: How often is helpful vs. annoying
- **Communication channels**: Where reminders should go

## ADHD Profile

- **Diagnosis status**: Diagnosed, suspected, or exploring
- **Current supports**: Medication, therapy, coaching (context only)
- **Common pitfalls**: Social media, hyperfocus traps, specific time-sinks
- **Strategies that work**: What has helped in the past

## Communication Style

- **Tone preference**: Gentle prompts vs. direct reminders
- **Response to body doubling**: Helpful, neutral, or unhelpful
- **Accountability sensitivity**: What language feels supportive vs. judgmental
- **Celebration style**: How they like wins acknowledged

## Patterns to Track

- **Routine adherence**: Which routines stick, which don't
- **Time estimation accuracy**: Do they over/underestimate?
- **Trigger situations**: What causes overwhelm or paralysis
- **Recovery patterns**: What helps them get back on track
